Planno is a specialized leadership scheduling workflow platform designed for interview coordination and meeting scheduling inside organizational leadership structures of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

The application is already under development.

Development Philosophy:

- Improve reliability, security, and workflow correctness.
- Preserve working user behavior unless a clear improvement is required.
- Refactor by extracting logic rather than rewriting working subsystems.

Product Scope:

- Leadership scheduling workflow management.
- Booking conflict safety.
- Multi-level organizational isolation.
- Interview tracking.
- Reminder notification workflows.

Planno is intended to remain a standalone scheduling workflow tool and should not depend on external church backend systems or APIs.

Primary Technical Goals:

- Pipeline-level booking safety.
- Database-enforced conflict prevention.
- Role-based authorization enforcement.
- Audit logging visibility.
- UX clarity and accessibility.

Target Users:

- Organizational leadership members and authorized participants within church workflow environments.
