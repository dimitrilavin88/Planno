# SMS Opt-In Consent Attestation  
## Verbal Consent – Meeting Reminders & Booking Confirmations

**Purpose:** This document attests to our process for obtaining and documenting consent to send transactional SMS (booking confirmations and meeting reminders) when opt-in is obtained verbally or in person.

---

### 1. Use of SMS

We send the following types of messages only:

- **Booking confirmation** – One SMS immediately after a client confirms a meeting booking (date, time, and a note that they will receive a reminder).
- **Meeting reminder** – One SMS approximately 15 minutes before the scheduled meeting start time.

We do not send marketing, promotions, or other non-transactional messages via SMS.

---

### 2. Opt-In Type

Consent is obtained **verbally** (e.g., by phone) and/or **in person** when the client provides their phone number for the purpose of receiving meeting-related messages. No marketing or unrelated messages are sent.

---

### 3. Consent Process

- Before sending any SMS, we obtain the client’s phone number and clear agreement to receive meeting confirmations and reminders.
- Consent may be obtained:
  - **By phone:** The client is informed that we will send a confirmation and a reminder by text; they confirm (e.g., “Yes,” “That’s fine,” “Please do”).
  - **In person:** The client provides their number and agrees to receive confirmations and reminders by text.
  - **Via our booking flow:** The client enters their phone number on our booking form with the understanding that they will receive a confirmation and a reminder; submission of the form constitutes consent for these messages only.

We do not add phone numbers to SMS messaging without the individual’s knowledge or agreement for these transactional messages.

---

### 4. Documentation of Consent

We maintain evidence of consent as follows:

- **Booking form submissions:** For online bookings, consent is documented by the client’s submission of the booking form after entering their phone number, with the form context making clear that they will receive confirmation and reminder texts.
- **Verbal / in-person:** Where consent is given verbally or in person, we rely on (a) a defined script or process that staff follow to obtain consent, and (b) internal records (e.g., CRM notes, booking records) indicating that the client agreed to receive these messages. Where call recordings are used, consent may also be evidenced by the recording.

We retain these records for at least the period required by our data retention policy and applicable law.

---

### 5. Unsubscribe / Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message or by contacting us directly. We honor opt-out requests promptly and do not send further SMS after opt-out except as required by law.

---

### 6. Attestation

I attest that:

- The above accurately describes our use of SMS and our process for obtaining consent for meeting confirmations and reminders.
- We only send the types of messages described in section 1.
- We obtain consent (verbal, in person, or via the booking form as described) before sending SMS.
- We maintain documentation of consent as described in section 4 and will provide it upon reasonable request by Twilio or a carrier for compliance or verification purposes.
