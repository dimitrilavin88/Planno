# SMS Opt-In Attestation (Verbal Consent)

**Company / Service:** Planno (scheduling and meeting reminders)  
**Date:** _____________________

---

## Purpose

This document attests to our process for obtaining and documenting consent before sending SMS messages (booking confirmations and meeting reminders) via our toll-free number. We use **verbal** and **web form** opt-in as described below.

---

## Opt-In Method

- **Primary:** Customer provides their phone number and agrees to receive messages when they complete a booking on our scheduling link (web form). By submitting the booking form with a phone number, they consent to receive:
  - One immediate booking confirmation SMS
  - One reminder SMS approximately 15 minutes before the scheduled meeting
- **Secondary (if applicable):** Customer may also provide their phone number and give verbal consent (e.g., by phone or in person) to receive the same transactional messages. In such cases, consent is confirmed during the conversation before any message is sent.

---

## Proof of Consent

- **Web form:** Consent is evidenced by the customer’s submission of the booking form that includes a required phone number field and completion of the booking flow. We retain booking records (including phone number and timestamp) in our system.
- **Verbal:** When consent is obtained verbally, we document it via [choose one or more: call recordings / internal notes or CRM entry / script plus staff attestation]. We do not send marketing messages; only transactional confirmations and reminders related to the meeting the customer booked or agreed to.

---

## Commitment

We confirm that:

1. We only send SMS to individuals who have provided their phone number and agreed to receive meeting-related messages (via our booking form or verbal consent).
2. We do not send promotional or marketing SMS; only transactional booking confirmations and meeting reminders.
3. We retain evidence of consent (booking records and, where applicable, documentation of verbal consent) in accordance with our data retention policy and applicable law.
4. We honor opt-out requests and will remove or suppress phone numbers from messaging upon request.
